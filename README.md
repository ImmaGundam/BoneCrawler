## How to Play
- Dodge skeletons that hone in and attack you
- Upgrade chests give 3 options and are random. They appear every 10 skeletons<br>
  - Heal - Restore 1 Heart - Max at 5
  - Bigger Sword - Upgrade sword - Unlimited
  - Magic Shield - Creates a damaging shockwave on contact - shockwaves Stack
  - Increase Speed - Faster Movement - No limit
  Choose and stack your upgrades wisely to survive!

- Normal skeletons: defeated in 1 hit - but get quicker and more spawn as your score gets higher (1 point)
- Big Skeletons: spawn every 20 kills, take 3 hits to defeat ( 5 points & drop a heart )
- Floating Wizard Skull: spawn every 50 points and Shoot Fireballs ( 3 points )
- Each hit to the player causes a half heart of damage
- Zone 2 key drops at 100 kills
