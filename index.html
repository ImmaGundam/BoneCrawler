BONECRAWLER
CONTROLS
DESKTOP:
ARROWS / WASD = MOVE
SPACE / LEFT CLICK = ATTACK
ENTER / E = INTERACT
ESC = PAUSE GAME (SKIPS DIALOG)
DODGE — SHIFT
MOBILE:
D-PAD = MOVE
B BUTTON = ATTACK
E / USE BUTTON — INTERACT
A BUTTON — DODGE
TAP HUD — USE POTION
The player name holds Cheat-codes!
- immagundam (Godmode + Boost)
- Link (Start Buffed)
- whydragons (Secret Dragon 1)
- itsasecret (Start in Secret Zone 1)
- devsecret (Start in Secret Zone 2)
- zone2 & zone3 (Start in Zone)
Scoreboard is stored locally as a cookie. Clear cache to reset.
DISPLAY ZOOM
HOW TO PLAY
CHEST ROLLS
Each chest contains 3 random choices.
An upgrade, a full heart or Points.
HEART — HEAL 1 HEART [MAX 5]
SWORD — BIGGER BLADE
SPEED — INCREASE SPEED
STEP — IMPROVE DODGE
MAGIC SHIELD — ABSORB HIT & BURSTS IN SHOCKWAVE
Upgrades stack by increasing stats.
Shields stack by increasing shockwave distance.
Pick Wisely!
The world has secrets. Find them. (Try looking for breakable objects)
ITEMS:
HALF HEART — HEALS 1/2 HEART
FULL HEART — HEALS 1 HEART
HEALTH POTION — HEALS 1 HEART
MASTER SWORD - +5 BLADE & WHIRLWIND SLASH
SKILLS:
SHADOW STEP - Dodge at greater length with brief invincibility
WHIRLWIND SLASH - Creates a 360 attack radius. Charge & release attack.
MIRROR - REFLECT ALL ATTACKS (HOLD TO ACTIVATE) Coming Soon!
